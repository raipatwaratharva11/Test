Why Grind

We believe that a truly great coffee shop isn't just about the beans—though ours are exclusively single-origin and ethically sourced. It's about the environment. Grind was designed from the ground up to be a haven for designers, writers, and creatives. A place where the aesthetic is considered, the lighting is just right, and the wifi never drops.

  • • Ethically sourced, single-origin beans
  • • Designed for long working sessions
  • • Quiet hours, 2pm–5pm, Mon–Fri
